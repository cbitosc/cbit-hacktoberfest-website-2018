Why organize a Hacktoberfest-themed Hackathon?

  • Increase awareness of open source in your community
  • Enjoy contributing to open source in a social setting
  • Meet project owners, maintainers, contributors, and community members in person
  • Learn about the open source ecosystem: from how to start an open source project, to marketing your project, sustaining growth, and troubleshooting and maintenance

What's this for?

Hacktoberfest mainly focuses on intrested open source contributors who are fascinated about solving issues and problems on real time working projects. This fest gives a good exposure to the students as well as developers on Hackathons and Open Source competitions like GSoC, AWS Hackathons, SIH and get to know many opportunities in Computer Science Industry.

About Us

COSC is an open source focused community based in Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology - Hyderabad. We are a group of open source enthusiasts who use and promote open source software.
